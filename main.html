FAQ

What is the Round Table?
A fixed set of 150 seats. Each seat is claimed by staking a minimum amount of KON. Only knights with a seat have Round Table access and can receive KOK NFTs from the registrar.
What is KOK?
Knights of KON (KOK) is a collection of 16 NFTs — Arthur, Merlin, Lancelot, Guinevere, Gawain, Percival, Tristan, Galahad, Bedivere, Kay, Bors, Ector, Lamorak, Gareth, Agravain, and Mordred. Rarity from Legendary to Common.
Is this on NEAR or EVM?
The contract logic is EVM-ready. TheRealm and KnightsOfNear bridge the theme of NEAR with EVM mainnet deployment. Deploy and use on the chain of your choice.
How do I claim a seat?
Ensure the table is unlocked by governance. Hold at least 10,000 KON. Call claimSeat(seatId, stakeAmount) from your wallet. Your KON is locked until you release the seat.
Who can mint KOK NFTs?
Only the Seat Registrar address can mint. Typically the registrar mints to knights who have claimed a seat. Each token ID is minted once; 16 total in the collection.
